About

What is this?

The Interactive Scrum Guide is a web-based tool for exploring Agile and Scrum framework guides in an interactive way. Each guide is enriched with inline keyword tags that let you filter, search, and navigate directly to the topics you care about — such as Sprint Planning, Product Backlog, or Definition of Done.

What is it for?

Whether you are studying for a Scrum certification, onboarding a new team, or just need a quick reference during a discussion, this tool helps you find the right section across multiple guides in seconds. Instead of reading an entire document, pick a tag and see every paragraph that relates to that concept — across the Scrum Guide, Kanban Guide, Nexus Guide, Scrum@Scale, and more.
